FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with NexGen.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects range from 4–12 weeks depending on complexity and scope. Starter websites typically take 3–4 weeks, while enterprise solutions may require 8–16 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline estimate during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes — all plans include post-launch support. Beyond the included period, we offer monthly retainer packages starting at $299/month for continued updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and priority support.

What technologies do you work with?

We work across the full modern stack: React, Next.js, Vue, Node.js, Python, Go, AWS, GCP, Azure, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and more. We recommend the best fit for your project's needs rather than a one-size-fits-all approach.

Can you work with our existing design system?

Absolutely. We're experienced in integrating with existing brand guidelines, design systems, and component libraries. We'll maintain consistency with your established visual identity while enhancing its implementation.

Do you sign NDAs and provide IP ownership?

Yes. We routinely sign NDAs before project discussions begin, and upon full payment, all intellectual property — code, designs, and assets — is fully transferred to you. We retain no licensing rights to your deliverables.

What is your payment structure?

We typically work on a 40/30/30 payment schedule: 40% upfront to begin, 30% at the mid-project milestone, and 30% upon final delivery and approval. Flexible arrangements are available for larger enterprise engagements.
